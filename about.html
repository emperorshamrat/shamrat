Who I Am

I am currently living in Mirpur-10, Dhaka, Bangladesh. Alongside my academic journey, I am working as an Executive Manager (EM) at Insignia Tours and Travel, a travel agency focused on assisting foreigners in exploring the beauty and culture of Bangladesh.

I have a deep passion for traveling and learning about different countries, cultures, traditions, and lifestyles. I enjoy understanding how people live around the world and how cultural diversity enriches humanity. I also love contributing to children’s education and believe that proper guidance at an early age shapes a better future.

My Goals & Vision

My future goal is to become a good and impactful teacher. I do not believe in conventional education alone. I want to promote a different kind of education — one that builds self-educated, responsible, and morally strong human beings.

My vision is to work for world peace and help people understand their true value, responsibilities, and potential. I enjoy social work and helping others, as I believe service to humanity is one of the greatest purposes of life.
